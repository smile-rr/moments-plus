## Information Collection

### Photos and Media

- The App accesses your photo library to organize and display your photos
- All processing happens locally on your device
- Photos are not uploaded to external servers

### Location Data

- The App reads location metadata from your photos (EXIF data)
- Apple's reverse geocoding service may be used to convert coordinates to place names

### Purchase Information

- Managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit
- We do not have access to your payment information

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No external servers store your personal data

## Third-Party Services

- Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)
- Apple MapKit (maps and geocoding)
