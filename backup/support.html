FAQ

How does Moments+ organize my photos?

Moments+ uses the time and location data embedded in your photos to automatically group them into meaningful moments. Photos taken around the same time and place are grouped together.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to external servers. We take your privacy seriously.

What is Moments+ Premium?

Premium unlocks unlimited moments, advanced map exploration, and voice notes. You can subscribe monthly or yearly.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your subscription.

Why does the app need photo access?

Moments+ needs access to your photo library to organize and display your photos. Without this permission, the app cannot function.
